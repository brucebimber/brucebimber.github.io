Advising

I typically advise about five graduate students and work with others through the Political Communication Working Group. My students have varied interests in political communication in the US and internationally, and they generally share a focus on digital media.

I do not anticipate taking on new graduate students for the cohort entering in Fall 2026.

I occasionally host postdoctoral scholars and pre-doctoral visitors from other institutions. I strongly discourage unsolicited proposals for an extended visit without any prior exchange or interaction.
